White Bread

Description:

This is another recipe I enjoy making for my wife.

I like to think it makes her life easier.

Ingredients:

  • 500g White Flour
  • 300g Tepid Water
  • 11g Kosher Salt
  • 5g Instant Yeast

Directions:

  1. Allow yeast to bloom for 10 minutes in the tepid water before adding remaining ingredients.
  2. Allow your stand mixer to beat up the dough for 10 minutes before allowing two 1 hour rounds of rising (broken up by deflating and shaping).
  3. Bake at 375F until the internal temperature hits 195F (approximately 30-40 minutes.)
  4. Wait and hour for the bread to cool and rest before slicing!
